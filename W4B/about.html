About Me

I've always been involved in projects that allow me to work hands-on with people and be creative. With more than 10 years of experience in video production, I have developed skills and knowledge in photography, videography, editing, animation, post-production, filming, lighting, etc. While working as a CNC operator I was inspired to learn 3d modeling through cadd software. Getting a deeper understanding of manufacturing has helped lead me into a more administrative role such as Marketing Coordinator. Which has given me great opportunities to gain new skills in both my career and personal life. Through this, I hope I will achieve more experience, skills and continue improving my talents. Learning is a passion of mines and teaching all that I know to those who come next.
